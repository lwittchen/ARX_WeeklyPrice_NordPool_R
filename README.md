# Content of the Term Paper
In this term paper I forecast the weekly electricity price from the Nord Pool Day-Ahead market with an ARX-model. I reproduce the model presented by Kristiansen (2014) and suggest extensions.

# Literature
Model based on: Kristiansen, T. (2014), ‘A time series spot price forecast model for the nord pool market’, International Journal of Electrical Power and Energy Systems Volume 61, S. 20–26. Denmark.

# Data
The data were kindly provided by Nord Pool AS and the Chair for Energy Economics at the University Duisburg-Essen.
